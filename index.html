About Us

The United SMEs Association of Kenya is a non-profit organization committed to helping Small & Medium Enterprises connect and promote their businesses for a more profitable and sustainable future.
The United SMEs Association of Kenya was formed out of a realization that severe problems were being faced by the SME sector in Kenya, which were not being addressed due to the lack of a strong representative organization

Advocacy and Policy Support

United SME's play a crucial role in advocating for policies that support the growth and success of small and medium enterprises. This could include lobbying for favorable tax policies, regulatory frameworks that are conducive to business growth, and other measures that make it easier for SMEs to access funding, markets, and other resources.

Why Choose Us

At United SME's, we understand the unique challenges faced by small and medium-sized enterprises. That's why we offer a range of services tailored specifically to help you grow your business and overcome obstacles.
From technology and innovation support to advocacy and policy assistance, we have the expertise and resources you need to succeed.

But what really sets us apart is our commitment to collaboration and community. We believe that by working together, we can achieve more than we ever could alone. That's why we bring SMEs together, to share knowledge, build networks, and create opportunities for growth.

So if you're looking for a partner who understands your needs, and who will work with you to achieve your goals, look no further than United SME's. Join us today, and together we can build a brighter future for your business.
